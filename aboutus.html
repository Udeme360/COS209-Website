MISSION AND VISION

Steeped in a tradition of excellence, YOLO Estate is on a completely new plane of quality, taste and splendor in the Nigerian Real Estate sector. Located in Ibeju-Lekki axis of Lekki Peninsula, in Lagos, YOLO has set a high benchmark for comfort and convenience in Nigerian Master planned communities. Designed to celebrate the grandeur of Nigeria's rich architectural heritage, YOLO features a complementary to our culture and modern lifestyle. Our homes are not just beautiful; they are practical and affordable for the discerning home buyer in Nigeria and Abroad.

AMENITIES & SERVICES

In addition to our exquisitely designed homes, YOLO is a master planned community that celebrates the classical Nigerian Urban designed concept of the Live-Work-Play community. Our estate offers leisure center including a gymnasium, pool, fitness center, tennis court and football pitch, public parks and gardens. There are also plans for a shopping center and schools. The estate boast designated and commercial area to include modern open air market, community center for parties and events and children's playground, complete with recreational furniture. • Concierge and janitorial services

• Waste Management services and fire hydrants
• Integrated CCTV and broadband internet for link with main gate and neighbors
